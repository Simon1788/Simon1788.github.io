Photos from Spain

As you'll not doubt spot, the theme of this part of the holiday was food, food, food. Trip Advisor served us particularly well in both Granada and Seville, taking us to some amazing and very reasonably priced Tapas places. A particular hightlight was the apple tiramisu (pictured). Who had any idea that coffee and apple could blend as flavours. The architecture was stunning too, a lot of it dating back to the Moorish rule in Southern Spain.

Photos from Iceland

The natural wonders of Iceland were absolutely breath-taking. When a rainbow bursting out of a waterfall becomes normal by the end of a holiday, you know that the scenery must have been amazing. We only visited for 3 days and Iceland had just stopped being the 'land of the midnight sun'. There were some specatacular sunsets and the Northern Lights even put in a brief appearance (albeit behind a fairly dense layer of cloud). I can not recommend Iceland enough as a holiday location and will definitely be returning soon.
